## **Scenario-Based & Calculation Questions**

### **21. Scenario: You are managing a software project with the following cash flows: Year 0: -$50,000, Year 1: $20,000, Year 2: $20,000, Year 3: $20,000. What is the payback period?**

B. 3 years
C. 2 years

B. Communication

D. Financial analysis
