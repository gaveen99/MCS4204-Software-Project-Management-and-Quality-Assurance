41.
If a project has the following cash flows: Year 0: -$100,000, Year 1: $30,000, Year 2: $40,000, Year 3: $50,000, what is the payback period?
A. 2.4 years
B. 2.6 years
C. 3.0 years
D. 2.0 years
Answer: B (Explanation: 100,000 - 30,000 - 40,000 = 30,000 remaining; 30,000/50,000 = 0.6; Total = 2.6 years)
